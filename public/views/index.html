Synopsis

"Nosferatu" is a 1922 vampire film based on Bram Stoker's "Dracula." Count Orlok, a vampire, moves to a small town, spreading terror and illness. Thomas Hutter, a real estate agent, discovers the count's true nature and tries to save his wife, Ellen. As fear grips the town, they seek ways to protect themselves. Ellen bravely confronts Count Orlok to stop his reign of terror. The film's atmospheric visuals and expressionistic style set the stage for vampire mythology. "Nosferatu" remains a timeless horror masterpiece, influencing the genre for generations.
